A Long Weekend in

Napa Valley

May 7 – 11, 2026

Day One

Arrival & Downtown Napa

Thursday, May 7

Afternoon

Archer Hotel Napa

Check-in · 1230 First Street, Napa

A luxurious boutique hotel nestled in the heart of downtown Napa's First Street, steps from over 60 tasting rooms, shops, and restaurants. Archer blends modern sophistication with wine country charm through curated art collections, locally sourced touches, and thoughtful amenities. Recognized by Condé Nast Traveler, AAA Four Diamond, and Forbes Travel Guide, the property features a rooftop spa, acclaimed dining, and rooms with private balconies overlooking wine country.

Lunch

Oxbow Public Market

Lunch & Exploration · 610 First Street, Napa

Napa Valley's beloved local gathering place for great food and wine, bringing together artisan producers, food vendors, and wine purveyors under one roof along the Napa River. The indoor marketplace celebrates the region's culinary culture with a curated mix of vendors offering everything from fresh produce and specialty foods to local wines. A vibrant, community-driven atmosphere perfect for grazing, discovering new flavors, and soaking in the local food scene.

Evening

Sky & Vine Rooftop

Drinks & Dinner · Atop the Archer Hotel

Perched six stories above downtown Napa, Sky & Vine is Napa Valley's favorite rooftop bar, offering sweeping valley views alongside seasonal fire pits and a relaxed, convivial atmosphere. The menu features thoughtful, shareable plates rooted in seasonal ingredients and a genuine love for bringing people together over fresh flavors and great company. An ideal spot for sunset cocktails or a leisurely evening overlooking wine country.

Day Two

Donum & Raymond

Friday, May 8

10:00 AM

The Donum Estate

Discover Experience · ~90 minutes · Carneros

A guided walking tour through Donum's culinary gardens, working farm, and select highlights of The Donum Art Collection — one of the world's largest accessible private sculpture collections, featuring over 60 monumental works by artists such as Ai Weiwei, Yayoi Kusama, Louise Bourgeois, and Keith Haring. The tour culminates in a seated tasting of Donum's single-vineyard Pinot Noir and Chardonnay, paired with seasonal canapés made from estate-sourced ingredients. A rare fusion of fine wine, world-class contemporary art, and regenerative farming set across 200 acres in Carneros.

2:00 PM

Raymond Vineyards

Winemaker For A Day · St. Helena

Live the dream of being a winemaker in Raymond's Crystal Cellar. This hands-on blending experience guides you through the art of crafting a Napa Valley Bordeaux-style red blend under expert guidance. Blend, bottle, and label your own custom creation to take home as a personalized vintage — with the option to reorder your signature blend anytime afterward. An immersive, creative experience at one of Napa's most distinctive and design-forward estates, currently celebrating its 50th anniversary.

Day Three

Schramsberg Day

Saturday, May 9

10:30 AM

Schramsberg Vineyards

Cave Tour with Classic Tasting · ~75 minutes

A guided journey through Schramsberg's historic underground caves — one of Napa Valley's most storied sparkling wine producers. The roughly 75-minute experience takes place entirely indoors within the atmospheric cave system, where you'll sample six limited-production sparkling wines and still wines from the Davies Vineyards label. An intimate, immersive experience that pairs rich winemaking history with exceptional bubbles.

Afternoon Optional

Castello di Amorosa

Tour & Tasting · Calistoga

An authentically styled 14th-century Tuscan castle in Calistoga — 107 rooms spanning 121,000 square feet, complete with a chapel, Knights Hall, Great Hall, and even a medieval torture chamber. The Diamond Estate Tour guides you through two upper levels of the castle while tasting reserve and current-release wines. A truly one-of-a-kind spectacle in Napa Valley — part winery, part architectural marvel. Alternatively, keep the afternoon open for exploring or relaxing.

Day Four

Mayacamas Day

Sunday, May 10

11:30 AM

Mayacamas Vineyards

Reserve Estate Tour & Tasting · ~90 minutes · Mount Veeder

A privately guided, in-depth exploration of one of Napa Valley's most historic mountain properties, founded in 1889. Set high on Mount Veeder, the 90-minute experience takes you through the estate grounds to discover its unique high-elevation terroir and over 135 years of winemaking history. The tour culminates in a curated tasting of both current releases and rare library vintages. Widely regarded as a legendary California wine estate — the intimate, appointment-only format amid mountaintop views makes this one of the valley's most contemplative and unhurried experiences.

Afternoon

Open Afternoon

Return from the mountain and enjoy the rest of the day at your own pace — perhaps a late lunch downtown, a stroll through Napa, or relaxing at the hotel before the last evening in wine country.

Day Five

Departure

Monday, May 11

Morning

Coffee & Breakfast Downtown

Start the morning with coffee and a leisurely breakfast in downtown Napa before heading home. An easy departure day to savor the last moments of wine country.
